<p><p>Structural Heart Imaging (SHI) focuses on visualizing and assessing heart structures to diagnose and manage cardiovascular conditions. Key market types include echocardiograms, which use ultrasound waves to create real-time images of the heart's chambers and valves, allowing for functional assessment. Angiograms, on the other hand, involve the use of contrast dye and X-ray imaging to visualize blood vessels and identify blockages or abnormalities. Both modalities are essential for planning interventions and monitoring structural heart diseases.</p></p>
