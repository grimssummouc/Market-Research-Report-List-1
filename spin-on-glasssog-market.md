<p><p>Spin-on Glass (SoG) is a type of dielectric material used in microelectronics, primarily for gap fill and insulation in semiconductor devices. The market can be divided into Hot-Temperature Spin and Normal Spin types. Hot-Temperature Spin involves the application of SoG at elevated temperatures to enhance properties such as density and thermal stability, making it suitable for advanced applications. Normal Spin, on the other hand, is applied at standard temperatures, focusing on ease of process and versatility across various semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
