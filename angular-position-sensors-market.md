<p><p>The Angular Position Sensors market is divided into two main types: Linear Angular Position Sensors and Rotary Angular Position Sensors. Linear Angular Position Sensors measure displacement along a straight line, providing accurate angular position data for applications requiring linear motion. In contrast, Rotary Angular Position Sensors gauge the angle of rotation, commonly used in robotics and automation to track the position of rotating components. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and industrial automation.</p></p>
