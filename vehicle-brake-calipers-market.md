<p><ul><li>Single Piston Caliper</li><li>Multi-Piston Caliper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle brake caliper market comprises two main types: single piston calipers and multi-piston calipers. Single piston calipers utilize a single piston to push the brake pads against the rotor, offering simplicity and reduced weight, making them ideal for smaller vehicles. In contrast, multi-piston calipers feature multiple pistons, providing enhanced braking performance and heat dissipation, suitable for high-performance or heavy-duty vehicles. Each type is designed to optimize braking efficiency and vehicle safety, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
