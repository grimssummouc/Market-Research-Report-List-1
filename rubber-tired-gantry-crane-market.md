<p><p>The Rubber Tired Gantry Crane (RTGC) market is categorized primarily into two types based on wheel configurations: 8-wheeler and 16-wheeler cranes. An 8-wheeler RTGC is typically lighter, more agile, and suited for limited space applications, optimizing maneuverability. Conversely, the 16-wheeler RTGC offers enhanced stability and load capacity, making it ideal for larger ports and heavy-duty operations. These cranes are essential for container terminal efficiencies, enabling the rapid movement and stacking of cargo containers in varying operational environments.</p></p>
