<p><ul><li>Hot Chamber Die Casting Machine</li><li>Cold Chamber Die Casting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnesium die casting machine market is primarily divided into two types: hot chamber and cold chamber die casting machines. Hot chamber machines are designed for metals with low melting points, like magnesium, allowing for rapid cycle times and efficient production. Conversely, cold chamber machines are used for higher melting point alloys, where molten metal is poured into the chamber manually, reducing contamination risks. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs based on the specific properties of magnesium.</p></p>
