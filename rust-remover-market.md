<p><p>The Rust Remover market is categorized into three main types based on their pH levels: acidity, alkaline, and neutral rust removers. Acidic rust removers typically contain strong acids that efficiently dissolve rust but may require careful handling due to their corrosive nature. Alkaline rust removers use base ingredients to neutralize rust, often being safer for various surfaces. Neutral rust removers maintain a balanced pH, providing a gentler option suitable for sensitive materials, offering effective rust removal with minimal risk of damage.</p></p>
