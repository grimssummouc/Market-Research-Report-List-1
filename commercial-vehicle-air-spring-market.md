<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Air Spring Market comprises three main types: Convoluted, Sleeve, and Rolling Lobe. Convoluted air springs feature a compact design with convoluted rubber bellows, ideal for space-saving applications. Sleeve type air springs consist of a cylindrical sleeve allowing for greater vertical movement and stability. Rolling lobe air springs utilize a rolling lobe mechanism that enhances load-carrying capacity and ride comfort, making them suitable for heavier vehicles. Each type serves distinct needs in commercial vehicle suspension systems, contributing to improved performance and safety.</p></p>
