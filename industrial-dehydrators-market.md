<p><strong>The Industrial Dehydrators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conduction Type</li><li>Convection Type</li><li>Radiation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial dehydrators market can be categorized into three types: conduction, convection, and radiation. Conduction type dehydrators utilize direct contact with heated surfaces to remove moisture from products, making them efficient for solid items. Convection type dehydrators employ heated air circulation to enhance drying, ideal for a range of materials. Radiation type dehydrators use infrared energy to dry products, providing quick and uniform moisture removal. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to various industrial drying needs and applications.</p></p>
