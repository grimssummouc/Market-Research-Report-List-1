<p><p>The nuclear facility decommissioning market encompasses several types, primarily focused on different reactor technologies. Pressurized Water Reactor (PWR) decommissioning involves the dismantling and safe disposal of components from PWRs, which use pressurized coolant. Boiling Water Reactor (BWR) decommissioning targets BWRs, where water boils in the reactor core. The "Others" category includes decommissioning of other reactor types, such as fast reactors or research reactors. Each market segment requires specialized techniques to ensure safety and regulatory compliance during the decommissioning process.</p></p>
