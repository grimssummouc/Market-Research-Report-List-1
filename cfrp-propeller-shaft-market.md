<p><p>The CFRP propeller shaft market is divided into two main types: single piece and two piece CFRP propeller shafts. Single piece shafts offer a streamlined design with fewer joints, resulting in reduced weight and improved strength, making them ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, two piece shafts consist of two segments connected by a coupling, allowing for easier installation and flexibility in design. Both types leverage the lightweight and high-strength properties of carbon fiber reinforced polymer, enhancing efficiency and durability in marine propulsion systems.</p></p>
