<p><p>The biodegradable cutlery market primarily consists of three key types: PLA (Polylactic Acid), CPLA (Crystalline PLA), and starch blends. PLA cutlery is made from fermented plant starch, offering a compostable alternative to plastic. CPLA is an advanced version, providing enhanced heat resistance, making it suitable for hot foods. Starch blends combine natural starches with other materials to create cost-effective, compostable options. Together, these types cater to the growing demand for eco-friendly dining solutions, reducing plastic waste and environmental impact.</p></p>
