<p><ul><li>Linear Encoder IC</li><li>Rotary Encoder IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The encoder IC market is segmented into linear and rotary encoder ICs, each serving distinct applications. Linear encoder ICs measure position along a straight line, often used in industrial automation, robotics, and measurement systems for precise movement tracking. In contrast, rotary encoder ICs track rotational position and speed, commonly found in motors, automation control, and consumer electronics. Both types enable accurate feedback in systems, enhancing performance in various sectors, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p></p>
