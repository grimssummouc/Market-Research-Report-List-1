<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Collapsible Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rack Assist Type (REPS)</li><li>Colum Assist Type (CEPS)</li><li>Pinion Assist Type (PEPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Collapsible Electric Power Steering (EPS) market comprises three main types: Rack Assist Type (REPS), Column Assist Type (CEPS), and Pinion Assist Type (PEPS). REPS integrates the motor with the steering rack, providing direct support for better handling. CEPS places the electric motor in the steering column, enhancing compactness and efficiency, often in smaller vehicles. PEPS features the motor within the pinion gear, offering improved responsiveness and adaptability, suitable for various vehicle designs. Each type caters to different vehicle dynamics and consumer preferences.</p></p>
