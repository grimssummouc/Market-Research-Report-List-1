<p><p>The hemodialysis device market encompasses three primary types: hemodialysis machines, water treatment systems, and dialyzers. Hemodialysis machines are essential for filtering and purifying blood, serving as the core equipment in treatment. Water treatment systems ensure the provision of high-quality water necessary for safe dialysis procedures, preventing contamination. Dialyzers, often referred to as artificial kidneys, facilitate the actual separation of waste and excess fluids from the blood during the hemodialysis process. Together, these components ensure effective renal replacement therapy.</p></p>
