<p><p>The Specialty PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication System) market is segmented into two primary types: On-Premise and Cloud solutions. On-Premise PACS involves installing software on local servers within healthcare facilities, offering control and data security but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud PACS utilizes remote servers hosted by service providers, offering scalability, cost-effectiveness, and easy access from various locations, making it ideal for organizations seeking flexibility in managing medical imaging data.</p></p>
