Vertical units occupy less floor space and are ideal for tight areas, making them suitable for high-rise buildings. Ceiling-mounted units are designed to be installed in ceilings, effectively conserving space and providing aesthetic benefits. Each type serves distinct applications based on spatial constraints and building design requirements.</p></p>
