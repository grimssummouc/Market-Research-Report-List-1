<p><strong>The Aluminium Car Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Casting</li><li>Forging</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminium car wheel market comprises three primary types: casting, forging, and others. Casting involves pouring molten aluminium into molds, allowing intricate designs and shapes but may result in slightly lower strength. Forging entails shaping solid aluminium through high pressure, resulting in wheels that are stronger, lighter, and more durable. The "Other" category includes alternative manufacturing processes, such as extrusion and flow forming, which cater to specific performance and aesthetic requirements, appealing to diverse consumer preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
