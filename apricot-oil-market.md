<p><p>The apricot oil market primarily comprises two extraction methods: solvent extraction and cold pressing. </p><p>Solvent extraction involves using chemical solvents to separate oil from apricot kernels, resulting in a higher yield but potentially affecting oil purity and quality. In contrast, the cold-pressed method mechanically extracts oil without heat, preserving nutrients and flavor, making it more desirable for health-conscious consumers. Market trends indicate a growing preference for cold-pressed apricot oil due to its organic appeal and superior health benefits.</p></p>
