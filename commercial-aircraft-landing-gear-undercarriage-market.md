<p><p>The commercial aircraft landing gear and undercarriage market comprises various types designed for specific operational needs. Strut landing gear provides robust support and is commonly used in larger aircraft. Rocker landing gear features a pivot mechanism for improved shock absorption, enhancing passenger comfort. Framed landing gear offers a lightweight and strong structure, ideal for smaller aircraft. Pontoon landing gear is designed for seaplanes, enabling landings on water, emphasizing versatility across different environments and flight operations.</p></p>
